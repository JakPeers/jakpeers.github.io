About

Awareness has been the root of many social problems. There are a lot of NGOs and projects all over Jakarta targeting certain needs of the society all over the world. However, lack of support makes it ineffective.

JakPeers aims to solve the problem by connecting volunteers all over Jakarta to the projects hosted by NGOs, governments, companies, even individuals.

JakPeers provides the kindhearted yet busy people the platform to volunteer and embrace humanity in a simple, effortless way.

nearby volunteering events hosted by NGOs, governments, companies or individuals.

Connect

Customize
